Refund & Cancellation Policy

Please note: All bookings for Sunyatee Retreat are final. No refunds are available once a transaction is completed.

Our retreats are planned with limited capacity and significant advanced logistics. Therefore, we maintain a strict cancellation policy.

1. No Refunds

Once a booking is confirmed and payment is processed, no refund will be issued under any circumstances, including non-attendance or early departure from the retreat.

2. Transfers

While we do not offer refunds, you may request to transfer your booking to another person at least 7 days before the retreat begins. Such requests must be sent to info@sunyatee.com and are subject to approval.

3. Retreat Cancellation

In the rare event that Sunyatee Retreat must cancel a session due to unforeseen circumstances, we will offer a full credit toward a future retreat date.
