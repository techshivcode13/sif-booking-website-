Shipping & Logistics Policy

Sunyatee Retreat provides experiential meditation services and retreats. As such, there are no physical products to be shipped.

1. Service Delivery

All services (accommodation, meals, and meditation training) are delivered on-site at the retreat location specified during booking. Your booking confirmation serves as your digital ticket.

2. Arrival Logistics

Participants are responsible for their own travel to the retreat location. Detailed instructions on how to reach the venue and check-in times will be sent via email after booking confirmation.

3. Digital Vouchers

Upon successful payment, you will receive a digital confirmation on the website. Please keep a screenshot or printout for verification at the venue.
